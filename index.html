Snowball

Backward-Forward Snowballing

Extract cited references and citing articles from any paper. Provide a DOI, a PDF, or both.

📄

+ PDF (drag or click)

Provide both DOI + PDF for best coverage. The tool cross-checks and merges results from APIs and PDF parsing.

Analyzing

Paste up to ~20 DOIs. Each will be queried via Crossref, OpenAlex & Semantic Scholar. Navigate results via tabs below.
